Project Overview

Problem

Diabetic Retinopathy is a progressive complication of diabetes that damages retinal blood vessels and may lead to vision loss if not detected at an early stage.

Solution

This system leverages the Xception Convolutional Neural Network to analyze retinal fundus images and classify disease severity levels with high precision, enabling timely medical intervention.
